BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application )

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) undergraduate programme is based on the guidelines and eligibility defined by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). BCA is a three-year programme that spans six semesters. It is designed to provide the introductory knowledge about computer applications. The master’s programme aims to shape computer professionals further with specializations in computer applications as well as domain specific knowledge. It prepares the students to face the challenges and avail opportunities in the IT industry. The syllabus is focused on the core fundamentals of computer science. BCA graduates can seek job opportunities in fields like software development, Web design, systems management, quality assurance and software testing, digital marketing and so on.



eligibility



A candidate seeking admission to 3 Years BCA Course should be Passed 10+2 examination with Mathematics as compulsory subjects with at least 50% marks and 45% marks in case of SC, ST, OBC category of Madhya Pradesh in aggregate of any recognized board
