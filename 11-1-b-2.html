Masters in Commerce (M.Com.)

This is a postgraduate course offered for graduates in Commerce. This is a two-year course approved by UGC. It is a two year Course spread over 4 Semesters.

This Post Graduate program is generally focused on different disciplines like accounting, taxation, banking, actuarial science, business management, corporate governance, economics, statistics, financial management, financial planning, etc.

Core Competencies

  • Google online certification course
  • AIMSR is the Local chapter of Swayam /NPTEL courses
  • Certification courses like Tally, NSE Certification Courses, MS-Office
  • Various Value Addition Courses like Digital Marketing, Business Analytics.
  • Microsoft Certification

key factor

  • Digital learning environment along with regular classroom teaching.
  • Hands-on experience and Certification in MS Excel, Tally Accounting, Capital Market, Taxation, etc.
  • Startup incubation center to handhold the students with entrepreneurial traits.
  • Live project based and case-based learning along with regular classroom teaching..
  • Regular Industry visit, Guest lectures of Industry Experts are arranged to familiarize students with the practical world.
  • Newspaper Review, Book Review and Movie Review are incorporated in regular teaching
  • Integrated Internship and Placement platforms provided for topmost companies like Wipro, TCS, HDFC Bank, L&T, etc.
  • In-house arrangement of classes of Communication Skills, Reasoning, Aptitude, GD, PI, etc. by the topmost trainers of the city.
    • and many more

eligibility

A Bachelor’s degree in Commerce with a minimum of 50% marks..
